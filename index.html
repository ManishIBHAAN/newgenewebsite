About Us

WHO ARE WE

Born in the summer of 2019, NewGene Security Solutions LLP has continually and successfully provided tailormade IT, security, detection, Protection, and authentication services to suit the needs and desires of our esteemed clients.

We offer valuable business solutions to our clients with a board of experienced consultants and professionals. We also are staunch believers in giving back to society.

We source our products from top-notch digital companies and provide some of the most advanced electronic security solutions to our customers. We are responsible for the entire electronic security system – right from installation, down to its use.

Our job doesn’t end at the installation of your devices. We are also responsible for their regular maintenance. We have a well-trained and efficient staff to handle all the system.

We keep a regular track of technological upgrades that get implemented regularly as and when the need arises. Newgene imparts regular training on the systems installed to the key members at the client’s place.
