THIS IS US

Surveillance technology for Karnataka State Police

For the protection of our protectors, The Karnataka State Police, we have installed CCTV surveillance cameras across the state. These include 7 central jails and 16 sub jails. Cameras were placed strategically throughout the interior and exterior of the building premises to achieve maximum coverage area. Video footage is searchable and can be exported as needed. Cameras are all networked, i.e., there is a dedicated server that handles all communication and recording.

Authentication of Biodegradable Garbage Bags

NewGenehas joined hands with the Indian Compostable Polymer Association (ICPA) on a large-scale authentication project. With growing consumerism demanding large-scale production of products, counterfeit products have become a primary concern for manufacturers and producers. To make a dent in the counterfeiter’s onslaught of fake and look-alike products in the market, encrypted QR codes were introduced on biodegradable laminates. With an efficient team of skilled workers, the project was completed within 24 hours at a production rate of 40-60 packets per minute. Today, countries all around the world have initiated using our bio compost bags that are authenticated and foolproof by the introduction of dynamic QR codes.We are proud to have initiated this movement towards eco-sensibility and climate awareness.

Installation of Biometrics
